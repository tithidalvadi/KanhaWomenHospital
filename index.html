About Us

Welcome To Kanha Women Hospital

Kanha Women Hospital is dedicated to providing compassionate and personalized healthcare services for women. Our mission is to support women through every stage of life — from adolescence to menopause — with expert medical care and a warm, comfortable environment.

Under the expert guidance of Dr. Nirmit Chaudhary, we offer a comprehensive range of women's health services including gynecology consultations, pregnancy care, high-risk obstetrics, PCOS management, and preventive wellness programs.

We believe every woman deserves healthcare that respects her individuality, addresses her specific needs, and empowers her to make informed decisions about her health and wellbeing.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions from our patients.

How do I book an appointment at Kanha Women Hospital?

+

You can book an appointment through our online booking form on this website, by calling us at 09157087391, or by visiting us directly at 1st Floor, Saffron Business Park, Jashoda Nagar, Ahmedabad. You'll receive a confirmation with a unique appointment ID.

What services does Kanha Women Hospital provide?

+

We offer comprehensive women's healthcare including gynecology consultations, pregnancy care, antenatal and postnatal care, high-risk pregnancy management, PCOS treatment, women's wellness checkups, menstrual health management, family planning counseling, and menopause care.

Do you provide complete pregnancy care from start to finish?

+

Yes, we provide end-to-end maternity care — from early pregnancy confirmation and antenatal monitoring through to normal or caesarean delivery and postnatal recovery. Dr. Nirmit Chaudhary will guide you through every step of your pregnancy journey.

What are the consultation timings?

+

Our consultation hours are Monday to Saturday from 9:00 AM to 12:30 PM and 4:00 PM to 7:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays except for emergencies. We recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred time slot.

How can I contact the hospital for urgent queries?

+

For urgent queries, please call us at 09157087391. You can also reach us via WhatsApp using the button on this page, or visit us directly at 1st Floor, Saffron Business Park, Behind Fire Station, Jashoda Nagar, Ahmedabad – 382445.

Is PCOS treatable? What treatment options are available?

+

Yes, PCOS is very manageable with the right treatment plan. Dr. Nirmit Chaudhary offers comprehensive PCOS management including hormonal therapy, lifestyle and dietary counseling, insulin sensitizers, and fertility support where required. Each plan is tailored to the individual patient.
